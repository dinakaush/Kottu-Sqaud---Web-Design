ABOUT US


WANNA HEAR OUR STORY ?

Well, it all starts with four friends and their love for Kottu.Yatta, Don, Wait-for-it (HIMYM reference) and Poobala, as they are affectionately known, all ventured into the food industry back in October 2010. Their vision was to let customers mix and match the carb, veggies, and protein, to create the perfect kottu for their tastebuds. KOTTU SQUAD started with just Kottu and have introduced a wide range of delish appetizers and drinks.

Kottu is such a beloved Sri Lankan meal and everyone has their preferred type of kottu. It's tough to cater to everyone's varied tastes but that's what the create your kottu option is for!

We've come a long way since October 2010.We couldn't have done it without you, our loyal group of kottu lovers!

In appreciation, we are running promos and offers on our social media platforms. If you have an idea that can help us grow and be better, get in touch!
